EDSO Logo

EDSO (Edgy-Sophisticated) is a women's fashion brand that offers additional editorial on site that strongly supports themes of women empowerement, individualism, and high fashion. I feel the logo I designed captures all of these ideas. The shape is abstract to represent individualism. Moreover, it is reminiscent of both a pair of women's heels - fashion - and a naked women laying down - appreciation of female body/women empowerement. Lastly, the Serif font coupled with the abstract logo design, bring home the idea of "Edgy-Sophisticated."

Capsulate Logo

Capsulate is the first successful web-app launched under LIVEINEVERYNOW. The idea is on brand with LIVEINEVERYNOW. in that it actively supports the idea of living in the present/now. Capuslate's core function is a photo-memoir app. It encourages users to take one picture every day that acknowledges the beauty in their day. The photos are then collected in a scalable display, in hopes that one can look back at everyday of their year and feel that each day was different and not a "blur". The logo is new and fresh like all new start-ups lol but also a floating time capsule, as Capsulate hopes to embody a digital time capsule of daily beauty.
